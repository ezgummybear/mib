Cheesy Fries

ingredients

For the Cheesy Fries:

  • 900 grams of frozen french fries
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of bacon bits
  • 1/4 cup of sliced green onions
  • 1/2 cup of ranch dressing
  • oil
  • parsley

How i make them

Cooking Instructions (Fries) :

  1. Preheat oil to 180°C. Carefully place the fries into the oil.
  2. Fry for about 3 - 4 minutes or until light golden colour.
  3. Remove the fries from the oil and place it on a kitchen paper to remove excess oil.
  4. Season the fries with salt.

Cooking Instructions (Melting Cheese) :

  1. Preheat oven to 80°C.
  2. Place the fries on a pan and sprinkle the cheddar and mozzarella on it.
  3. Bake the fries and cheese in the oven for 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted
  4. Remove the pan from the oven and sprinkle bacon bits, green onions and parsley over the fries.
  5. Serve it with some ranch dressing on the side or drizzle it directly on the fries.
