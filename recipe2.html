Little Jimmy

ingredients

For the Little Jimmy:

  • 500 grams of ground boneless pork shoulder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 - 3 slices of bacon stripes
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of tabasco sauce
  • 2 buns
  • Some sugar
  • Some butter
  • Some bacon bits
  • Some fresh lettuce
  • Sprinkle of salt and pepper

How i make them

Cooking Instructions (Patty and Sauce) :

  1. For the patty, mix the grounded pork with 1 egg yolk and 2 finely chopped garlic. Then, season it with salt and pepper.
  2. Form patty shapes and let it set aside.
  3. For the sauce, mix the mayonnaise and tomato sauce first. Follow by a little bit of sugar.
  4. Add in tabasco sauce to taste. Beware of making it too spicy.
  5. Add in the bacon bits to create more texture.

Cooking Instructions (Frying and Plating)

  1. Prepare a flat pan and put it on a stove on medium heat. Place the butter in and let it heat up evenly.
  2. Cook the patty and bacon stripes. 2-3 minutes each side or until evenly browned.
  3. Place the patty on a bun with lettuce.
  4. Place bacon stripes on the patty.
  5. Drizzle the homemade sauce on the whole burger.
  6. Serve it with a cold lemonade to cool off the spicyness.
