Beef Truck

ingredients

For the Beef Truck:

  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 2 spoon of mustard
  • 1 spoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 spoon tomato sauce
  • 2 buns
  • Some butter
  • Some pickles
  • Slices of tomato
  • Some fresh lettuce
  • Sprinkle of salt and pepper

How i make them

Cooking Instructions (Patty) :

  1. Crack an egg and remove the egg white and keep the yolk.
  2. Chopped the onions finely.
  3. Mix the ground beef with the yolk and chopped onions. Mix for 30 seconds.
  4. Season the mixture with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour in the mustard, Worcestershire sauce, tomato sauce into the mixture. Mix until it is even.

Cooking Instructions (Frying and Plating)

  1. Prepare a flat pan and put it on a stove on medium heat.
  2. Place the butter in the stove and wait for it to heat up.
  3. Cook the patty. 2 - 3 minute each side.
  4. Place the cheese on the patty and cover the pan for a few seconds.
  5. Serve the patty in a bun with lettuce,tomato and pickle.
